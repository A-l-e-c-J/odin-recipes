Crepes Recipes

Description

These 30-minute dessert crepes recipe is perfect for dessert, and....nope, that's just about it. Fluffy, buttery, and any other adjective you can think of can apply to these wonderful little crepes.

Want a dessert recipe that'll knock Grandma's socks off? Weirdo. Get some help.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 and 1/3r cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  1. Whisk together all ingredients until smooth
  2. Heat that skillet and grease it up
  3. 1 Pour 3 tablespoons of batter in there until you got yerself a crepe
  4. Repeat until you run out of batter
